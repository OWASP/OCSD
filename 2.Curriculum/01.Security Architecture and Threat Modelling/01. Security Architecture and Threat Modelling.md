### Secure Architecture/Design Principles

* Foundational coding practices that contribute to security: DRY, KISS, YAGNI, etc.
* Minimize Attack Surface
* Fail Securely (unless failing **safely** matters more)
* Apply Layered Protections (Defense-in-Depth)
* Segregation of Duties
* Least Privilege
  * Minimum Necessary Third-Party Access
* Economy of Mechanism
* Complete Mediation
